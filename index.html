Welcome to R-140, the innovative mobile application dedicated to fostering volunteerism and philanthropy through the synergy of physical fitness and community challenges.

About IEEE R10 140 Initiative

At R-140, we believe in the transformative power of combining personal wellness with collective action for the betterment of society. Our platform provides a unique opportunity for individuals and teams to engage in activities such as walking, jogging, running, or swimming, while simultaneously contributing to charitable causes. Whether you're a seasoned athlete or just embarking on your fitness journey, R-140 welcomes participants of all levels to join our movement. By committing to exercise goals and completing challenges, users not only enhance their well-being but also make a meaningful impact on their communities. Join us today in making a difference, one step or stroke at a time.

FAQ

Who all can participate in this Initiative?

This Initiative is open to all, not limited to IEEE Members. 

Can we participate in team?

Yes, a person can participate as an individual or in a team.

What are the team size?

There is no limit for the team size.

How much activities can be recorded per day?

Either 25km or upto 3 activities per day can be added without any verification, after the limit, the activity is added only after verifying by admins.

Which all activities are allowed?

Most of the activities are allowed, not limited to running, jogging, swimming, etc.
